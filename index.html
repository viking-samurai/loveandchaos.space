About

Have you ever perceived something so amazing that it made you question reality itself? Or, maybe you've explored something so thoroughly that you wound up walking right through the Veil itself and didn't even notice until you got there? I did that once myself and I enjoyed it so much that I decided to stay. Hi, my name is Ryan Degen, and I'm a practicing Shaman under the guidance of Maestro Drago Jax.

My Journey through the Veil started in my early twenties, when I swore a vow of love while on teaching contract in Seoul, South Korea. I had just commited adultery for the first and only time, and I was so digusted with myself that I vowed to never do it again. Unbeknownst to me at the time was that I was being trafficked by swingers, and that this vow would play out in the great city of Calgary, Alberta, some sixteen years later.

It's been an excellent ride so far, despite almost getting killed for my faith at one point, and I can now say from personal experience that few things in this Universe trump true conviction. That's a lesson I'm blessed to explain from this side of reality, still, as opposed to the next. So join me as I share my experiences with faith and conviction, as well as discuss my recovery from PTSD using mostly a mixture of Psilocybin, LSD, Cannabis, and beer. Hopefully you'll all laugh, but maybe there're a few out there who will draw inspiration from my experiences for their own struggles as well. May the Archangel Lucifer bless you all.

Cheers!
